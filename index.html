The Makhana Journey

From the depths of Bihar's ponds to your snack box — every step crafted with love

🌿
Step 01
Harvesting from Sacred Ponds
Makhana (Fox Nuts) grow in the still, shallow ponds of Bihar — India's makhana capital. Farmers wade into chest-deep water to hand-pick the lotus seeds, usually at dawn when the water is calmest.
🌿
☀
☀
Step 02
Sun Drying
The freshly collected lotus seeds are spread on clean mats and dried under the scorching sun for 1–2 days. This reduces moisture and prepares them for the next stage of processing.
🔥
Step 03
The Magic Roasting
Dried seeds are roasted in clay pots or iron pans at very high temperatures. At the perfect moment, the outer shell pops open — just like popcorn — revealing the soft, white, fluffy makhana inside! 🎉
🔥
📷
📊
Step 04
Grading & Quality Check
Skilled workers carefully sort and grade the popped makhana by size and quality. Only the finest, crunchiest pops make the cut for MunchJoy. Broken or small ones are separated out at this stage.
🍳
Step 05
Flavouring with Love
This is where the magic happens! Premium quality makhana is slow-roasted in just the right amount of cold-pressed oil and tossed with our secret spice blends — Tangy Tomato, Say Cheese, Peri-Peri, and Cream & Onion. Each batch is crafted by hand.
🍳
📦
📦
Step 06
Packed Fresh, Delivered with Joy
Finally, the freshly flavoured makhana is sealed in airtight packs to lock in the crunch and freshness. Every pack of MunchJoy is your ticket to a guilt-free, delicious snacking experience. 🎉
